10/22/2010 Larger ads coming soon

We will shortly support larger ads on Applifier to help you find more users to your applications faster.

Initially larger ads will be available to those applications that for some reason sending out more clicks than they are getting back. The large ads will help to balance this and return clicks back to those applications.

Shortly thereafter we will make it possible for you to use larger ads to promote launches of new games and features in order to rapidly find new users. The price of larger ads for these purposes will be announced later.
